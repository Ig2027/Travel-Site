Travel Times

September – September is one of the best times to travel to Seattle, the weather is still nice from Summer, but not too hot, fall colors are starting, and the whole environment is amazing!

October – Fall in Washington is absolutley gorgeous, the trees are changing, perfect for photgraphers, and Starbucks is starting to sell holiday drinks!

June – The weather in June is barely ever rainy or burning hot, most of the time June weather is pretty good! School is also ending so a perfect time for vacations!

April – UW is famous for their beautiful Cherry Blossums in the month of April, the weather is pretty good, a little rain but overall beautiful. Ocassionally, Ski slopes are also still open around this time.
