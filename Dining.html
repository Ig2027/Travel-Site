Dining

In the mood for pasta, dumplings, coffee, or just a quick snack, you'll be able to find it! Seattle is a very diverse city with over 2000 restaraunts with tons of different cuisines! Of course I wouldn't be able to list all two thousand, but done below are some of the bests! For more options check out Seattle Restaraunts Wikipedia!

Sky City - the space needle restaurant, a restaurant hundreds of feet in the air, spins, and has amazing food! For more information check out Sky City Wikipedia

Spinasse – Capitol Hill, amazing pasta, best Italian Restaurant. For more information check out Spinasse.com

Bateau – Steak House, many delicious choices

For more information check out Bateau.com

Canlis – Fine dining restaurant, one the greatest restaurants in America, luxurious, many choices. For more information check out Canlis.com

Le Pichet – French, located in Pike Place Market, amazing French classics, reasonably priced. For more information check out LePichet.com

Rasai – A luxurious Indian meal that is full of flavor and splendid. For more information check out Rasai.com

Starbucks – can be found almost everywhere in Seattle, a large café that has many options, if visiting Pike Place Market, you can even see the very first Starbucks! For more information check out Bateau.com

Cupcake Royale – For all those who have sweet tooths, come down to Cupcake Royale to tast one of the many cupcake flavors! For more information check out Starbucks.com

Molly Moons – many peoples favorites, 14 flavors, famous throughout Seattle, sundaes too. For more information check out MollyMoon.com

Lady Yum – Luxurious macaroons, found in malls, many flavors, beautiful stores located in many malls across Seattle, can walk around and pick whichever you like. For more information check out LadyYum.com
