Main Attractions and Travel Spots

          •	Space Needle - The famous Space Needle located in downtown Seattle is the main tourist attraction in Washington, the 605 foot tall observation 
          tower is open to everyone in Seattle for a small fee. Once you arrive at the top, enjoy the wonderful views and make sure to capture the moment 
          by taking pictures of Seattles fascinating skyline. Come for dinner and relax in the Skycity rotating restaraunt, if theres time!
          For more information check out  SpaceNeedle.com 

          •	Pike Place Market - One of Seattles many markets, home to the first ever Starbucks and many more exquisite restaraunts. The Pike Place Market 
          is full of many different types of farmers, craftsman, merchants, fishers, and more! So come on down for an amazing expierence including, food, 
          shopping, and an overall enjoyable day.
          For more information check out  PikePlaceMarket.com 

          •	Museum of Pop Culture - Calling all those who love Music and Movies, the Museum of Pop Culture explains the amazing pop culture history of 
          Seattle. For a small price of thirty dollars, you can see all the amazing exhibits museum founded by Microsofts co-founder Paul Allen! Located 
          just around the corner from the Space Needle, MoPop is an amazing attraction for all tourists traveling to Seattle. 
          For more information check out  MoPop.org 

          •	Woodland Park Zoo - The most famous zoo in Seattle, perfect for families with younger kids, or anyone with an interest in animals! Hundreds 
          of different species, and over a thousand animals. Along with many, many animals, the zoo also offers different perfomrances including, concerts 
          and shows with the animals! Woodland Park Zoo would be an amazing place to visit if you find yourslef in Seattle with nothing to do!
          For more information check out  WoodlandParkZoo.com 

          •	Museum of Flight - Plane Enthusiasts, Aerospace Lovers, the Museum of Flight has tons of aircrafts to see admire! About a twenty dollar tickets 
          for each person as of 2023, eighteen for youth, and free for children under four. So many fun things to see if you have an interest in flight, and 
          if you are looking for even more, you can take a quick drive up to Everett for a tour of the boeing factory!
          For more information check out  MuseumOfFlight.com 
          
          •	Pacific Science Center - Probably the most fun attraction in this list, the Pacific Science Center is an amazing experience for children of all ages, 
          and most adults! There are tons of activities and exhibits that can be seen there, including, water activities, shows, dinosaurs, space, giant chess
          boards, IMAX theatres, planetarium, butterfly room, and even Laser Shows! There are so many fun science exhibits to see, for a price of 30 dollars
          per person. 
          For more information check out  PacificScienceCenter.org 

          •     Spheres - The Amazon Spheres located right in the center of Seattle is a peaceful, calm, and beautiful environment to take a break in after a long 
          day of exploring Seattle. The tranquil vibe of the Spheres is fascilitated by the hundreds of plants and the large windows! There is also a restaraunt 
          where you can get a coffee to enjoy as you take a sit or walk through the plant exhibits located on the main floor! The only downside is the Spheres are 
          only open to the public on Saturdays, but available to all badged Amazon employees whenever they are open. 
          For more information check out  SeattleSpheres.com
