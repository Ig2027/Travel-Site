Shopping!

          •	Pike Place Market – many different stores, restaurants, has the first ever Starbucks, most famous market in Seattle, when ordering 
          fish get ready for it to be chucked at you
          For more information check out  PikePlaceMarket.org 

          •	Pacific Place – 5 floors, huge mall, has many high-end brands, tiffany & Co example, many dining options, theater, live music events, 
          Nordstrom extension
          For more information check out  PacificPlace.com 

          •	Georgetown Trailer Park Mall – made up of trailers, pop up shops, every weekend, coffee and cookies, Shape of you filmed in Georgetown, 
          vintage items, handmade crafts, collectables
          For more information check out  GeorgetownTrailerParkMall.com 

          •	University Village – open air market, shopping and dining options, many famous brands, different types of shopping and dining, second 
          ever Starbucks
          For more information check out  UVillage.com 

          •	Elliot Bay Book Company – multi level book store, cozy café inside, events including author readings, collectable editions
          For more information check out  ElliotBayBook.com 

          •	Bellevue Square Mall – A popular mall among many in Seattle, in the middle of city of Bellevue, lots of surrounding restaraunts and stores,
          if visiting around Christmas, stop by to watch the snowflake lane parade, perfect for families!
          For more information check out  BellevueCollection.com
